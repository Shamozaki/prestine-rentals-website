Prestine Rentals specializes in managing Serviced Accommodations and Houses in Multiple Occupation (HMOs). Our team of professionals from Cardiff University expertly navigates the complexities of property management, addressing challenges such as tenant management, refurbishment costs, and fluctuating incomes for property owners.


In response to rising living expenses for tenants, we offer guaranteed rental income opportunities to property owners, freeing them from the typical hassles of management. This approach provides a stable financial environment for owners and allows tenants to enjoy premium, economically feasible living conditions.


As a connector, we streamline processes and improve satisfaction for both property owners and tenants, ensuring swift and beneficial experiences for all parties involved.

Our housemates are youthful, energetic professionals working at top-tier companies in Cardiff. We use a strict selection process to ensure they are courteous and professional, with excellent references from past landlords.
